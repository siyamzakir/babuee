About Babuee

We are a small but “gigantic” thinking Event Management company. We are vibrant, energetic, but mindful. We have personality traits that make DY.o – highly organized, time conscious and efficient. We embrace a “we’ve got this” attitude that demonstrates the importance we place on being responsible at every stage of the process.
